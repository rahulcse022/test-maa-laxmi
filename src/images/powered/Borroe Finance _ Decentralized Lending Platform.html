The Need for Borroe

Challenge

Web3 represents the next evolution of the Internet, where value is rightfully shared with its creators and It's clear that legacy systems used by traditional funding providers are no longer suited to the evolving landscape, impeding the industry's overall advancement.

Traditional invoice factoring, financing or discounting problems:

* High costs
* Lack of automation
* No confidentiality
* Increased possibility of fraud
* Compliance restraints
* No real-time tracking of invoices
* Poor security
* Lengthy approval process

Web3 content creators, such as NFT artists, writers, musicians, digital creators and Web3 platform providers, often face cash flow challenges due to the nature of their work. They may have irregular income streams or face delays in receiving payments from clients or platforms.

Solution

With Borroe, businesses can quickly raise upfront cash by minting Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs) representing future and outstanding invoices and fees, which are sold at a discount price on the marketplace. The AI-driven platform utilises cryptocurrency, blockchain, tokenization, and smart contracts to enable a faster and more efficient working capital raising process.

How It Works

The platform integrates AI-powered risk assessment, secure blockchain technology, and streamlined payment solutions, providing a safe and efficient fundraising process. This encourages buyers to easily sell and trade future recurring revenue NFTs directly with each other on secondary markets, fostering a truly peer-to-peer ecosystem.

Sellers can offer large discounts and other incentives to encourage the community to fund their requests. Follow the path from seller, marketplace to buyer below.
